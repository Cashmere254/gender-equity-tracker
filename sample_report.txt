Quarter 1 Leadership Lab Impact Report 2026

Amina joined the leadership workshop in January 2026. After completing the programme, she reports feeling significantly more confident speaking in her community. She now leads the youth advocacy group in her village and inspires other girls to take initiative and assert their voices.

Grace received a scholarship to study at the University of Nairobi. Her mentor visits monthly to support her academic journey. She has excelled in her first semester and is now mentoring three junior students.

Fatuma disclosed that she experienced gender-based violence at home. She is now receiving counselling and protective support. She reports feeling safer and believes her rights are now being upheld by the community support network.

The savings circle has grown to 15 women. Each member now earns supplemental income from their small businesses. Three members have secured formal employment through the job placement programme.

The community network has expanded. Participants collaborate on advocacy projects and are creating positive social change in their area.
