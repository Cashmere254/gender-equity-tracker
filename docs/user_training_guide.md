# GEI Tracker — User Training Guide
For: M&E Officers, Programme Managers, and Donors at Akili Dada

## 1. Getting Started

### Accessing the System
Open your browser and go to: [LIVE_URL]
Click 'Sign In' if you have an account, or 'Get Started' to create a new one.

### Creating Your Account
1. Click 'Get Started — Sign Up'
2. Fill in your name, organisational email, and choose a password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Select your role: M&E Officer | Programme Manager | Donor
4. Click 'Create Account' — you will be redirected to Sign In

### Forgot Your Password?
Click 'Forgot your password?' on the Sign In page.
Enter your email — a reset link will be sent to your inbox.
Click the link, set a new password, and sign in.

## 2. For M&E Officers — Uploading Programme Reports
1. Sign in and click 'Upload Report' in the left sidebar
2. Drag your file (PDF, Word, or text) into the upload zone, or click Browse
3. Select the Programme from the dropdown
4. Select the Source Type (Focus Group, Field Notes, etc.)
5. Click 'Submit for Analysis'
6. The system extracts text, splits into paragraphs, and runs AI analysis (5–30 seconds)
7. Return to the Dashboard to see KPI cards updated with new evidence

## 3. Understanding the Dashboard
Each KPI card shows:
- KPI name and category (e.g. Leadership Confidence — Leadership)
- Number of narratives that matched this KPI
- AI Confidence %: how strongly the evidence matches the KPI keywords
- Sentiment: Positive (green) or Negative (red) — overall tone of narratives

A confidence of 60% means 6 out of 10 keywords for that KPI were found.
Always review source narratives before citing AI results in donor reports.

## 4. The Evidence Library
Click 'Evidence Library' to see all narrative chunks.
Each row shows text preview, source document, and matched KPIs.
Click any row to expand and read the full narrative.
Use the Programme filter to focus on one programme at a time.

## 5. Building a Donor Report
1. Click 'Report Builder' in the sidebar
2. Select the Programme from the Properties panel (right side)
3. Drag sections from the left panel onto the canvas:
   Cover Page → Executive Summary → KPI Grid → Narrative Block
4. Reorder sections by dragging — remove with the X button
5. Click 'Save Draft' to preserve your work
6. Click 'Export PDF' for immediate download
7. Click 'Export DOCX' for a Word document (save first)

## 6. For Donors — Viewing Reports
Donors see: Dashboard (read-only KPI data) and Evidence Library.
To access a shared report, ask your M&E Officer to export and send it to you.

## 7. For Programme Managers
You can view the Dashboard and Analytics pages.
Uploading and building reports is handled by M&E Officers.

## 8. Changing Your Password
Click 'Settings' in the sidebar.
Under 'Change Password': enter your current password and new password.
Click 'Update Password'.

## 9. Interpreting AI Results — Important Guidance
The AI pipeline is a decision-support tool, not a replacement for professional judgment.
Always verify KPI matches by reading the source narratives.
A high confidence score means strong keyword matching — not guaranteed accuracy.
Negative sentiment may reflect important programme challenges, not failure.
If a relevant narrative has no KPI match, the keyword dictionary can be expanded
by contacting your system administrator.
